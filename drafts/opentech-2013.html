OpenTech 2013

After recently attending the brilliant OpenTech conference in London my brain is full of fresh, occasionally terrifying, knowledge.

Firstly I have to recommend everyone to go read Bill Thomson's excellent "State of the Intersection". While I think there's plenty of over-exaggerations which were raised during the brief Q&A afterwards I do largely agree with his sentiment. Particularly his insightful reflection on the Luddites which seems very pertinent to me.
